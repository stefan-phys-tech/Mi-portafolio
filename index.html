Topological Quantum Materials

From Theory to Simulation

1. Introduction

This work explores the field of topological quantum materials, covering quantum insulators through the study of diverse quantized Hall Effects, both theoretically and practically via computer-based condensed matter simulation.

2. Theoretical Framework

Topological Insulators & Hall Effect

The study delves into the Integer Quantum Hall Effect (IQHE) where Hall conductance is quantized. We analyzed the role of the Berry Phase and the Chern Number.

BHZ Model

We focused on the Bernevig-Hughes-Zhang (BHZ) model for HgTe/CdTe quantum wells.

3. Computational Simulation

Using Python and the kwant library, we implemented the effective BHZ Hamiltonian.

Authors: Bru Serra, Marcel Vilaró, Stefan Giurgiu | UPC Download Full PDF

Scientific Report: ICIQ Research Stay

Uranyl Speciation in Carbonate Media

1. Introduction

Polyoxometalates (POMs) are complex molecular nanoclusters. This project applied the POMSimulator software to model the speciation of uranyl in carbonate media.

2. Methodology

  • Preparation: Modeling using Amsterdam Density Functional (ADF).
  • Simulation: Calculation of formation constants using POMsimulator.

Impact of Acid Rain on Crops

Experimental Research in Alcover

1. Introduction & Context

Air pollution and acid rain pose significant risks to local agriculture. This work investigates if acid rain is a real risk factor for wheat plantations in Alcover.

2. Experimental Methodology

  • Monitoring: pH and conductivity of real rain water.
  • Artificial Atmosphere: Controlled environment for gas acidification.
  • Germination: Effect of acidity on wheat seed germination rates.
